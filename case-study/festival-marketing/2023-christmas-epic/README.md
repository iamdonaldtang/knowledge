# 2023 Christmas Epic

For important festivals such as Christmas, many projects will invest a lot of resources during the festival campaign, leading to fierce competition. A reasonable threshold for campaign is crucial, because excessive thresholds may lead to low user participation. The purpose of the campaign must be clear and the task of your campaign is streamlined.
