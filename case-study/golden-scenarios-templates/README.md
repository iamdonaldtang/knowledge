# Golden Scenarios Templates

### TaskOn Growth Practical Guide: Golden Scenarios Templates to Empower the Entire Project Lifecycle

In the rapidly changing market environment of Web3, how to efficiently acquire users, increase activity and realize value conversion is the core challenge faced by every project team.

To this end, the TaskOn operations team, based on the platform's massive amount of successful activity data, has deeply analyzed the common needs at different stages of project development and meticulously crafted this "Collection of Scenario-Based Growth Templates . " This collection covers high-frequency operational scenarios, from new project cold starts, maintaining community activity, and token holder incentives to product feature promotion.

These templates are not just a collection of tasks, but rather proven growth strategies. No matter what stage of your project you are in, you can directly reuse these optimized activity configurations to achieve "One-Click Deployment" and "High-Efficiency Conversion" with minimal trial and error costs.

We hope this template will become part of your growth arsenal, helping you accurately reach your target users on the TaskOn platform and unleash the maximum potential of your projects.
