# Education and Training/Product Adoption

#### Target

• Increase user understanding of the project, lower the barrier to entry for users to use the product, and improve the usage rate of core functions.

#### Recommended Tools

• Community (TaskChain) + lucky wheel + Milestone

#### Recommended Configuration

• **TaskChain 1 (Introductory Understanding):**

￮ Task: Read the project white paper/watch the introductory video.

￮ Task: Answer 3 simple quizzes

￮ Reward: Points

• T**askChain 2 (Initial Interactive Experience):**

￮ Tasks: Sign in and other basic interactive tasks

￮ Task: Complete a basic interaction.

￮ Reward: Points

• **TaskChain 3 (Deep Use):**

￮ Task: Use advanced features such as staking, cross-chain functionality, and providing liquidity.

￮ Task: Write and share your user experience (POW).

￮ Reward: Points

#### Recommended reward methods

• **Lucky Wheel (30% of Budget)**

￮ Token + Discord Role + NFT

• **Milestone (70% of Budget)**

￮ Rewards: Token + Discord Role + NFT.

#### Select Eligibility

1\. Choose two conditions, at least one of which must be met.

2\. X recommends 10 followers, at least 1 month age account and a non-default background restriction on profiles.

3\. POH is recommended to be greater than 20 points.
