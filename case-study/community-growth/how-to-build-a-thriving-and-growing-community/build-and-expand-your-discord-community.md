# Build and Expand Your Discord Community

### How to Build and Expand Your Discord Community with TaskOn?

TaskOn's Discord features are designed to assist you in achieving community growth and flexible community management, ultimately enhancing user loyalty and creating a more robust and interactive Discord community.

* **Discord Bot**

TaskOn Discord Bot serves as a powerful assistant for your community growth and management. By executing automated tasks, it provides efficient management and interactive experiences for your Discord server. From verifying incoming users to distributing rewards for activities, TaskOn Bot focuses on enhancing community vitality, delivering personalized services, and boosting user engagement.

[_Before launching TaskOn activities, consider adding the TaskOn Discord Bot to your community, making subsequent operations more streamlined and user-friendly._](https://taskoncommunitys-organization.gitbook.io/entity-hub-for-business-end/function-cooperation/discord-bot-setting)

![](https://lh7-us.googleusercontent.com/4sRSU3st0DF17V94c1sky4IkYEQLVJrXTXJd5um4ktXyOVLpte41u8bda6jmmPTRIIz535T8wUNACtmkSXXAzEfw8UTdpwuPlUY6FBx9eaWAhCs5g3jeoYtZ1G-of_XxAytSARiV2K0eBuof3VXx1JY)

Once you've configured the bot, click on "`Create`" input the basic event details, and click on "`Reward`".

* **Reward Type: Discord Role**

TaskOn also supports using Discord Role as event rewards. Firstly, it provides users with visible badges, enhancing their social status. Secondly, by distributing specific roles, you can establish a sense of hierarchy within the community, motivating users to participate more actively. Most importantly, Discord roles as rewards are a lightweight and instantly visible incentive, directly reflecting a user's contributions and engagement in the community, encouraging more participation.

[_Tips: Apart from Discord Role, you can choose Token, NFT, Whitelist, Points as rewards. TaskOn's reward system supports compound rewards, allowing you to leverage your resources to attract more user participation._](https://taskoncommunitys-organization.gitbook.io/entity-hub-for-business-end/tutorial/rewards)

On the Eligible page, you can define the conditions for users to participate in the event, enhancing the quality of participants and mitigating the risk of users from certain regions.
