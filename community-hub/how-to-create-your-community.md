# How to create your Community?

In `TaskOn.xyz`, click `For Projects`, then select Create Community.

After logging into your account, follow the system prompts to enter your Official Website, upload the relevant logo, and provide other required information.

## Verified Community

If your registered community has not yet been verified, make sure to complete the verification process promptly.

Community verification helps prevent impersonation of your community.

Only communities with a verification badge will gain greater exposure and earn user trust.

Additionally, before setting up Quest events, if your community is not verified, your Quest events will not be displayed on the homepage.

## Customize Displays

You can customize the frontend display to align with your project’s visual branding needs, including themes, logo (avatars, ideally your project logo), background image, background effects, community introduction, TGE information, and more. You can choose which features to display on the frontend based on built-in templates.

<figure><img src="https://lh7-rt.googleusercontent.com/docsz/AD_4nXdY72c00EErP5jSyRP-oCCHYITOusHAO1FVG7KblKgMQXLSLx9HBAg8K0Yde-kvUBUb8HqtfeZTXtFDAOTF0tYsp1toZh_hOaSMHw7gfMBj2iETRdfRMupa_k7o0S3fdKMFENlZPA?key=0Ugs8HwP_vZfQwQiqIZ9vA" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

**Theme**: Customize the background color and display frame color scheme to better match your project’s primary color palette or visual preferences.

**Logo**: Click to upload your project logo.

**Header**: Upload a background image and configure dynamic effects.

**Community Intro**: Upload images to share any information you want users to know. These images will be displayed in the banner slot.

<figure><img src="https://lh7-rt.googleusercontent.com/docsz/AD_4nXfMcd3YFj-307D75wtC25lQjc0dFqegO1R8fXChgcamIwyqJ9VYDo843SUdAaO4MeB14mCcel6Y-VDbHUMcyZI2flKM0Pr2gTT8u_Y8m3hVEG_yLVHTjufV-hZEB5n73dcbmweqow?key=0Ugs8HwP_vZfQwQiqIZ9vA" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

**TGE Display**: Inform your community members about your airdrop schedule! This will be showcased prominently in the banner slot.

**Quest and Events**: Select up to one ongoing or upcoming Quest/event to feature on the homepage banner slot.

**Leaderboard**: Display the points leaderboard for community members, allowing them to check rankings at any time.

**Announcement**: The latest announcements will appear above the banner slot, promptly notifying community members of updates.
