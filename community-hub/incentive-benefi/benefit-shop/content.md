# Content

## Set Up

### Basic Info

1. Type of content: `File`, `Message`, `URL`.
2. Upload your content.

<figure><img src="../../../.gitbook/assets/image (106).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

### Set Redeem Rate

Please fill in the number, how many points are needed to redeem the content.

<figure><img src="../../../.gitbook/assets/image (107).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

### Quantity-limited (Optional)

If your whitelist is not unlimited or you want to control the quantity of whitelist redemptions, enter the maximum supply.

<figure><img src="../../../.gitbook/assets/image (108).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

### Time-limited (Optional)

If you want to set this benefit as time-limited, please enter the date.

<figure><img src="../../../.gitbook/assets/image (109).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

### Participation Requirement (Optional)

If you want only participants from a specific ranking range to qualify for this benefit, click "Participation Requirement", then select "Leaderboard" for the specific range of ranking.

<figure><img src="../../../.gitbook/assets/image (110).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

Click "`Next`".

<figure><img src="../../../.gitbook/assets/image (112).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

## Info

1. Enter your benefit `name`, load the `cover image`, and add the `description`.
2. Click "`Publish`".
