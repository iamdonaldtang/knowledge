# Introducing GTC Services

**1. What services does TaskOn GTC provide?**

TaskOn provides loyalty points for both off-chain and on-chain sources, along with tools for managing points programs. It offers smart task creation, multiple incentives like virtual and physical perks in the Benefit Shop, and various leaderboard options.&#x20;

TaskOn also allows you to personalize your airdrop policy based on a points system and offers easy point issuance through CSV data upload.&#x20;

Additionally, TaskOn provides traffic import options for different subscription plans, abundant data analysis and user profiling, custom domain support, and various support services including technical support, data export, customer services, and dedicated client services. TaskOn also offers community exposure control. These services ensure that you have the assistance you need to effectively utilize the TaskOn platform and address any inquiries or concerns that may arise.\
<br>

**2. What traffic import options are available?**

For the monthly plan, TaskOn provides banner promotion, priority display, and community shoutout.&#x20;

For the half-year plan, it includes a Twitter repost on the main account, banner promotion, priority display, and community shoutout at least six times.&#x20;

To unlock more traffic import benefits, you can contact the sales team.\
<br>

**3. What are the different plans and their costs for using GTC services?**

GTC services offer four plans: Free, Standard Plan, Premium Plan, and Enterprise Plan.&#x20;

The Free plan offers limited features but includes no boost or benefits.

The Standard Plan is a cost-effective option with basic features, and is suitable for projects seeking growth, including a 1-day boost and traffic import. It is available at monthly, half-year, and annual prices.&#x20;

The Premium Plan offers expanded features, automated task verification, a 3-day boost, and traffic import, making it suitable for rapidly growing projects. The Premium Plan is also available at monthly, half-year, and annual prices.

The Enterprise Plan provides comprehensive features, personalized support, a 5-day boost, and traffic import, tailored for large projects or organizations, with pricing options available on a monthly, half-year, and annual basis.<br>

**4. Can you provide more details on the pricing structure for the plans?**

The pricing for the Standard, Premium, and Enterprise Plans is available on a monthly, half-year, and annual basis.&#x20;

The specific costs for each plan will depend on the chosen subscription duration and the features included in the respective plan. It is recommended to review the pricing details provided by GTC to determine the exact costs associated with each plan based on your requirements.<br>
