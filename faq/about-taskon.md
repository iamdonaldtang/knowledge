# About TaskOn

**1. What is TaskOn?**

TaskOn is a Web3 Task Collaboration Platform that facilitates collaborative micro-tasking between humans and machines. It serves as a comprehensive platform for various scenarios in Marketing Growth and Community Engagement.



**2. What makes TaskOn unique?**

TaskOn stands out with its rapidly growing user base, providing a dynamic and diverse community for task collaboration. It offers a wide range of on-chain and off-chain task templates, which can be instantly verified through an automated process.&#x20;



**3. What types of tasks can be found on TaskOn?**

TaskOn covers a broad spectrum of tasks related to Marketing Growth and Community Engagement. This includes quests such as social media growth, data analysis, community engagement, on-chain transaction, and much more. There are numerous task templates available to cater to different needs and requirements.



**4.How does the verification process work for on-chain tasks?**

TaskOn employs an instant auto verification system for on-chain tasks. This process utilizes the power of blockchain technology to verify and validate task submissions automatically, ensuring transparency and efficiency in the verification process.



**5. Where can I find more information or get support?**

For additional information or support, you can visit the TaskOn website or reach out to the TaskOn team through our provided contacts. The customer support team will be ready to assist you with any inquiries or concerns you may have.<br>
