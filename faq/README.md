# FAQ

## Is TaskOn free?

Currently, other than our current Banner advertising placement, all functions on TaskOn are FREE!

## How to download QA answers?

Click `Entity Hub`, go to `Campaign`, locate the campaign and click `Please Upload Qualified Address Lists of 1 Tasks`.

<figure><img src="../.gitbook/assets/image (244).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

Click `Upload` link to open the page, then click `Campaign Task Data` to download QA answers.

<figure><img src="../.gitbook/assets/image (247).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

## How to download campaign datas?

Click `Entity Hub`, go to `Campaign`, locate the campaign.&#x20;

<figure><img src="../.gitbook/assets/image (263).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

Click the button in the lower right corner of the campaign to download campaign data.

## How to change campaign descriptions?

If your campaign doesn't start, go to `Campaign`, locate the campaign, click `Edit` icon to change the description.

## How to test campaign publication on TaskOn?

In the next minor version, TaskOn will add a test environment, allowing users to perform functional tests and publish test campaigns.

## Any suggestions for creators?

* First, we suggest that you need to get verified before creating the first campaign;
* Then, try our wonderful functions - deposit your rewards and select distribute by TaskOn to distributed rewards automatically;
* If your campaign includes PoW tasks, please be sure to upload the qualifiers list in time; If you choose to distribute rewards by yourself at the same time, please be sure to upload the winners list.
