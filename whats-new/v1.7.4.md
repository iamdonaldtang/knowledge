### Multiple Rewards

Using multiple combined rewards in activities can increase participant engagement and motivation, enhancing the attractiveness and effectiveness of the project. Diverse forms of rewards cater to different participants' needs, promoting multifaceted development of the activity. Moreover, multiple combined rewards can also improve the activity's sustainability and impact.
