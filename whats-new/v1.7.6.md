### Task Template: Join Community

In the upcoming TaskOn Marketing version, we are introducing a new task template: Join Community. With the Join Community template, you can create tasks within campaigns to encourage users to join the Community.

Through this template, you can efficiently convert traffic from the TaskOn Marketing version into active users within your own community, further expanding your community's reach and enhancing user engagement. This initiative aims to strengthen user loyalty and stickiness, ultimately contributing to the growth and success of your community.<br>
