# v1.7.2

<figure><img src="../.gitbook/assets/157.7.24 (1).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

We are thrilled to introduce **TaskOn v1.7.2** — a significant upgrade meticulously crafted to elevate the user experience for campaign creators and participants alike. This release introduces many innovative features, including **Campaign Templates** and **Account Merge**. Moreover, a multitude of experiential enhancements have been seamlessly integrated. For comprehensive insights, kindly peruse the information provided below.

## For Projects <a href="#id-56e7" id="id-56e7"></a>

### Campaign Templates <a href="#id-3fec" id="id-3fec"></a>

TaskOn has summarized the successful experiences of past campaigns and selected various campaign templates to help your project grow faster. These templates cover **brand awareness**, **social media growth**, **community growth and engagement**, **product growth**, **ecosystem collaboration**, and more. Regardless of your goal, we provide corresponding templates, allowing you to create similar campaigns with just a click.

By using TaskOn’s campaign templates, the process of launching campaigns becomes simple and efficient. The consistent structure ensures the standardization and professionalism of campaigns, thereby improving execution efficiency. The standardized process reduces the likelihood of errors, creating smoother conditions for the successful promotion of campaigns.

### Support for Starknet and Bitcoin Networks <a href="#bc44" id="bc44"></a>

For the **Starknet network**, TaskOn provide support for issuing **whitelist** rewards and **NFT verification**.\
For **Bitcoin Network**, TaskOn support projects in issuing **BRC-20 token** rewards, as well as **whitelist** rewards on the Bitcoin network.

With this update, activity creators now have a broader range of reward options and settings, enhancing the flexibility and efficiency of your growth plans.

### Solution Webpage <a href="#id-8876" id="id-8876"></a>

We have upgraded our homepage. Now, by simply clicking on the “**Solution”** in the navigation bar, you will be directed to a brand-new page. On this page, you can quickly understand how TaskOn addresses your growth challenges. Explore **successful growth cases** on TaskOn to gather inspiration for your own campaigns.

### New Task Template: NFT Holder <a href="#da45" id="da45"></a>

v1.7.2 introduces a new task template, the “NFT Holder” task. This task requires participants to hold specific NFTs, aiming to inspire their long-term support and involvement in the project. Through this task, projects can utilize activity rewards and privileges to encourage users to hold NFTs for an extended period, fostering stronger connections between the project and users while enhancing user loyalty.

### Notification Optimization <a href="#id-3211" id="id-3211"></a>

We have enhanced the notifications for Space Followers. Now, whenever you launch a new campaign, your followers will promptly receive messages in their Notification Box. This improvement is designed to ensure that your followers stay informed and actively engage with your latest updates.
