# Boost Hub

All our paid users enjoy **free marketing boost package**, which includes **Featured on Homepage**, **Banner Spotlight**, **Featured on Community Hub**, and **Multi-Channel (X/DC/TG) Promotions**.

_Standard subscribers_ can have **3** days/month of free marketing boost package, _Pro subscribers_ can have **9** days/month of free marketing boost package, and _Enterprise subscribers_ can have **21** days/month of free marketing boost package.

## How to Apply For Your Boost

In order to apply for your boost, you need to visit our Boost Hub. ​&#x20;

There are several options. First, you can have either your quest or community featured on our website. For Quest, you can choose between Homepage and Quest Banner; for Community, you can have it featured on Community Banner. ​&#x20;

To use your boost, click "Book" on the right side. Subsequently, you can choose your preferred date.
