### **Distribute NFT**

The NFT distribution feature enables projects to explore innovative branding strategies and boost user engagement. TaskOn currently supports three methods for distributing NFTs to users: _Airdrop_, _Claim_, and _Mint_.

* **Airdrop**: Directly send NFTs to a specific address list, requiring payment of gas fees. Each airdrop supports up to 120 NFTs.
* **Claim**: Users must pay gas fees to claim NFTs on TaskOn.
* **Mint**: Users pay gas fees to mint NFTs on TaskOn (with the option to earn revenue from minted NFTs).

Steps: Click “Distribute NFT to Users.”

### **Distribution Management**

The distribution management feature allows you to efficiently monitor the performance of all Key Opinion Leaders (KOLs) in specific tasks. With this tool, you can easily compare the effectiveness of each KOL.

After publishing a Quest, in the Distribution Management tool, click _Distribute Your Quests Now_ in the top right corner.

In the _Quest Link_ section, select the event you want to promote. In the _Where_ section below, choose X or YouTube.

In the _Who_ section, select a KOL or enter the KOL’s @username and configure the corresponding _Cost_.

Click _Add_ to include multiple KOLs.<br>

If the desired KOL is not in the list, you can manually input their details. If you don’t have a specific list, click _Find more KOLs_ to access the TaskOn Help Center, where we can assist in submitting your requirements to find additional KOLs.<br>

### Batch Reward Distribution

The batch reward distribution platform allows you to distribute tokens or NFTs to target users in bulk using the airdrop tool. Complete your desired airdrop in three simple steps: select the tokens/NFTs to distribute, upload the wallet addresses of the target users, and submit and sign the transaction in your wallet.<br>

Here, you can execute your airdrop plan or distribute task rewards.
