# Referral Program

Invite communities to join TaskOn and earn a commission of 10%+ on subscription fees, while the invited communities receive a 5% discount.

Click "+" icon, and share the code/link with others.

### Commission Rate

**Base Rewards: Earn 10% commission on subscription fees.**

| Plan            | Semiannually  | Annually |
| --------------- | ------------- | -------- |
| Standard Plan   | 96.00\*2      | 170.40   |
| Pro Plan        | 240.00\*2     | 430.80   |
| Enterprise Plan |               | 2000.00  |

**Additional Rewards: 2%\*(N-3) for the Nth invited community (N>3)**

| Plan            | Semiannually | Annually                                                                             |
| --------------- | ------------ | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------ |
| Standard Plan   | 19.20\*(N-3) | 34.08\*(N-3)                                                                         |
| Pro Plan        | 48.00(N-3)   | 86.16\*(N-3)                                                                         |
| Enterprise Plan |              | Cap: 50% of subscription fee for Standard/Pro Plan and 2,000 USD for Enterprise Plan |

e.g.

Assuming you sequentially invited A, B, C, D, E, where:&#x20;

A & B: Standard Plan (Annually)&#x20;

C: Pro Plan (Semiannually)

D: Pro plan (Semiannually)&#x20;

E: Pro Plan (Annually)&#x20;

then your rewards:&#x20;

A: $170.40 USD&#x20;

B: $170.40 USD

C: $240 USD (six-month later, another $240 USD)&#x20;

D: $288 USD ( =240 + 48 \* (4-3), six-month later, another $288 USD)&#x20;

E: $603.12 USD ( =430.8 + 86.16 \*（5-3) )

### Referral Rules

1. Invited community owners must register through the invitation link or enter the invitation code during registration to be considered as invited by the referrer.
2. Invitation Reward Amounts:&#x20;

1）For the referrer:\
a) You will earn a referral commission from each invited community, amounting to 10% of their first-year subscription fee. The more communities you invite, the greater your rewards.\
b) You'll also earn additional 2%\*(N-3) commission for the Nth communities you invite. (N>3)&#x20;



2）For the invited community owners:\
Communities invited will enjoy a 5% discount on their first-year subscription fee if they subscribe a semi-annual or annual plan. This discount will be deducted directly from the payment amount at checkout.

\
3）For you to receive the invitation reward, the communities you invite must subscribe to semi-annual or annual plans. Monthly plans are not eligible for commissions.

3. Distribution of Referral Rewards: You can apply to claim rewards through the 'Your Referrals' module on this page after the invited communities have paid for their subscriptions.
