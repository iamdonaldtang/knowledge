# Boost Hub

To increase visibility and participation in your event, you can apply for a boost to showcase it in the Banner slot.\
All paid users can enjoy a specific number of free marketing boost packages, which include Homepage Featured, Banner Spotlight, Community Hub Featured, and multi-channel (X/DC/TG) promotions.

* Standard subscribers receive a 3-day free marketing boost package per month.
* Professional subscribers receive a 9-day free marketing boost package per month.
* Enterprise subscribers receive a 21-day free marketing boost package per month.
