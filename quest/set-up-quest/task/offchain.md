# OffChain

Tasks include joining communities, engaging on platforms like `X`, `Discord,` `Telegram`, `YouTube`, `Facebook`, `Instagram`, `Reddit`, visiting websites, accessing `Twitch` channels, learning and testing, QA sessions, participating in AMAs, and other knowledge-based tasks. Task activities typically involve following, reposting, navigating to web pages for learning, or answering quizzes.

When setting up a related Task, simply fill in the corresponding information in the relevant template based on your content needs.

In addition to social media tasks, `OffChain` primarily focuses on designated interactions, such as visiting the official website, completing a Survey, etc. Simply specify the detailed requirements in the Task Description.
