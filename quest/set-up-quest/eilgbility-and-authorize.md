# Eilgbility\&Authorize

## Eilgbility：

Fine-tuned eligibility settings help project creators better control participant quality, improving task effectiveness and data authenticity.

&#x20;Only users meeting your requirements are eligible to participate. This tool combines anti-bot measures with targeted user selection to filter out bots and attract the desired participants.

### Anti-Bot Settings:

&#x20;Twitter verification and on-chain verification can be used to implement anti-bot measures through on-chain footprint conditions.

### Targeted User Selection:

Filter participants with conditions such as eligible wallet addresses, Discord roles, NFT holders, minimum token balance addresses, or participation restricted to residents of specific countries to meet your needs.

&#x20;Setting participation for or excluding residents of specific countries can help create Quests compliant with regulatory policies.
