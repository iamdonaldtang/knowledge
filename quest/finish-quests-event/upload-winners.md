# Upload Winners

If you select `Manually` in `How to draw winners`, you need to upload the winner list after the campaign ends.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (229).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

1. Click `Entity Hub`, go to `Campaign`, locate the campaign and click `Upload Winner List`.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (158).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

2. Click `Download Qualifiers List` to download the data list with users’ addresses, then choose the winners’ addresses from the list.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (166).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

3. Click `Download Template` at the bottom left corner of the page to get the standard file, fill in the addresses you get from the data list.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (193).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

4. Click `Upload qualified addresses` to upload your list file, then click `Confirm` at the bottom right corner of the page and you've successfully uploaded the list.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (180).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>
