# Deposit Rewards

TaskOn `Deposit Rewards` Function reduces the operation of frequently setting rewards as well as the manpower and time cost of issuing rewards for a single campaign, and can also stimulate user participation with automatic reward distribution.

1. Get into `Entity Hub`, go to `Assets`, click `Deposit Now` if you never deposit before. For now, you can deposit `Tokens` & `NFTs` as your rewards distributed automatically.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (172).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

When you click `Deposit`, you will get a popup like this:

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (213).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

If you deposit NFTs, you will get into:

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (268).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

2. Click `Please select network,` pick the network your assets on.

\*_Now, we support Ethereum, BNB Chain & Polygon._

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (195).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

3. If your token is already listed, you can click `Pick from list` to choose or input the token name to search.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (154).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

If your token isn't listed or you want to deposit your NFTs, you need click `Add Token` and input your token contract address.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (258).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

4. Input the amount of the token you want to deposit, choose the wallet and click `Deposit Now` to confirm.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (235).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (233).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

5. If you have deposited, you will find it listed on `Assets` where you can check the current balance:

* The amount of `Available` shows how many you can use in the next campaign.
* The amount of `Freezed` shows the tokens and NFTs you have deposited in the current campaign.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (184).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

And you can click `Transaction History` to check all transactions, including how many you have deposited and how many you have already expended in the previous campaigns.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (225).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

6. If you need to withdraw the tokens or NFTs deposited before, click `Withdraw` to get into a popup.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (239).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

You will get into a popup like this:

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (267).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

For NFT withdraw:

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (228).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

Choose which network, what and how many tokens or NFTs and which wallet you want to withdraw, then conform and wait until you receive your assets.
