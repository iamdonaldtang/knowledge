# How to Top Up Your Gas Balance

To enhance the user experience, you can deposit Gas Fees into the Gas Station to subsidize your TaskOn tasks and events, covering gas costs for users.

While most TaskOn tasks do not require gas fees, for Quests involving on-chain tasks or cases where winners need to pay gas fees to claim rewards, subsidizing gas fees can reduce user participation costs and boost engagement.

The process for subsidizing gas fees is straightforward:

Click “`Gas Station`.”

Select the Network, choose the Token, enter the deposit amount, select your wallet, review the information, and click Top Up.

<figure><img src="https://lh7-rt.googleusercontent.com/docsz/AD_4nXew-yNmn1e5-GvTUWZcL6SqKpHNA9Tb1BDCjo4VBIQuVyUUkiCLM7Hfwo7h96bRUhE8cSzatReApdQzDshgSWi_gt_8IKrYMtIuLRJqeul1tlQ4vGpQzBKr3bXM7osjRM0cm5d6?key=0Ugs8HwP_vZfQwQiqIZ9vA" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

Tip: To enable project creators to seamlessly create multiple gas-free tasks or events, they can pre-deposit a certain amount of gas fees in the Gas Station. This eliminates the need for repeated deposits when designing subsequent gas-free tasks or events.

If you’re unsure about the total gas fees for a task or event, click the “Calculator” in the top right corner. Enter the token value to display the result.

Alternatively, select the reward type and input the number of claims to estimate the gas fee value.

Wait for the deposit to be successfully processed.

<figure><img src="https://lh7-rt.googleusercontent.com/docsz/AD_4nXcZC8Vzn3YIsEQNLpCxr_Mb4QxcmWNddp-v4jDsy1upOofV1C53HD942JFrmNL8fzeLJg3gG2vauL2zrSAIwbk99IZi3pHUZI9teQ1zoTGbk-4JmfDvPy_ZpenL9Wut-GbF9xs?key=0Ugs8HwP_vZfQwQiqIZ9vA" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

Once sufficient gas fees are deposited, you can create gas-free tasks or events.
